Spinach and banana smoothie

Smoothie to cheer you up! Healthy choice for a snack or breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup plain soy milk

Steps

  1. Blend soy milk and spinach together in a blender until smooth. Add banana and pulse until thoroughly blended.
