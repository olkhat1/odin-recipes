Lasagna

Fast lasagna for the week

You will only need 30min to prepare, and 30min in the oven

Ingredients

  • 1kg Minced meat
  • 2 x Chopped onions
  • 3 x Garlic cloves
  • 1 pack of Pasta plates
  • 2 x Mashed tomato containers
  • 1 x Container of lasagna cheese
  • 500g of grated cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta plates on the side while preparing everything else
  2. Chop the onions and garlic
  3. Put some olive oil in the frying pan and add the chopped onions and garlic
  4. After 30sec, add the minced meat and cook it until done
  5. Add the mashed tomatos and some season in your own liking
  6. When everything is done, put the lasagna together with the lasagna cheese and put it in the oven on 200C
